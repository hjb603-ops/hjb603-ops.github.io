About Me

It has pushed me to become more independent, disciplined, and focused on improving my skills every day.

I’m particularly interested in web development and software systems. I enjoy understanding how technology works behind the scenes and turning ideas into working software. How This Links to Computer Architecture and Databases

This portfolio also connects to topics from my wider Computer Science studies.

Even though this is a static website, it still depends on real computing hardware. When a visitor loads the site, requests are handled by servers running on processors, memory, and storage systems. Learning computer architecture helps me understand performance, efficiency, and the wider system-level context behind software.

Databases

This portfolio is not database-driven, but many future applications are. Studying databases has helped me think about how information should be structured, stored, retrieved, and related. That is directly useful when planning tools such as budgeting systems, trackers, and other data-focused applications.
