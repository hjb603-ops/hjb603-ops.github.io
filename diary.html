Production Diary

This page records the main improvements made to my website and explains how those changes improved usability, structure, and presentation.

Entry 1 — Initial portfolio build

Date: 6 March 2026

What I did: I created the first version of my GitHub Pages portfolio and added core sections such as About, Projects, Skills, and Contact.

Entry 2 — Visual and usability improvements

Date: 6 March 2026

What I changed: I redesigned the website using a more professional layout with a hero section, card components, improved spacing, clearer buttons, and consistent navigation.

Why it matters: These changes make the site easier to scan, visually cleaner, and more consistent for the user.

Entry 3 — Adding the CS Maths page

Date: 6 March 2026

What I changed: I created a separate maths page containing an interactive binary and decimal converter using JavaScript.

Why it matters: This gave the portfolio a stronger technical element and showed how a simple computer science maths concept can be turned into a usable tool.

Entry 4 — Making projects more professional

Date: 6 March 2026

What I changed: I expanded my project descriptions so they include summaries, technologies, my contribution, and one challenge solved.

Why it matters: This makes the portfolio more useful for academic review and gives visitors a clearer picture of my skills and thinking.
