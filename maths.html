Explanation of the Maths

Binary is a base-2 number system that uses only 0 and 1. Decimal is the base-10 system used in everyday life.

To convert binary to decimal, each digit is multiplied by a power of 2 based on its position from right to left. The results are then added together.

For example, 1010 in binary means 1×2³ + 0×2² + 1×2¹ + 0×2⁰, which equals 8 + 0 + 2 + 0. This gives the decimal answer 10.
